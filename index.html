FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about starting coding for adults and kids.

Do I need prior experience to start coding as an adult?
No! Our courses are designed for beginners with no prior experience. You will learn step by step with guided exercises.
Can my child start learning coding if they are under 10?
Absolutely! We offer Scratch courses designed for kids to learn logic and creativity in a playful way.
Do I get support after completing the course?
Yes! We provide continuous mentorship and guidance even after the course ends.
Is coding too difficult for someone with no tech background?
Not at all! Our courses are designed for absolute beginners. Step by step, you'll gain confidence and understanding.
Will my child get bored learning coding?
Never! Our interactive Scratch projects make learning fun and engaging, keeping kids curious and motivated.
I’m worried I won’t have time to learn coding regularly. What should I do?
Don’t worry! Our courses are flexible and include short, structured lessons that fit into any schedule.
What if I make mistakes or don’t understand something?
Mistakes are part of learning! You’ll have mentorship and support to guide you until you fully understand each concept.
Is it too late to start learning programming as an adult?
It’s never too late! Many adults start from scratch and successfully build coding skills and career opportunities.
How do I know if my child is ready for coding?
If your child is curious, enjoys solving problems, or likes games and puzzles, they are ready to start learning coding in a fun way.
